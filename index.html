About Me

My name is Feizl Hassan. I'm a passionate graphic designer and web developer from Accra, Ghana. While I've always loved designing, even as a kid, my love for web development was discovered during my college years while studying publishing studies. Despite the focus on print media in my degree, I was captivated by the endless possibilities of the web as a publishing medium.

But my journey into web development would be long and challenging. I'm not gonna sugarcoat it – it was difficult. Learning to code is difficult, and even moreso if you lack the proper learning tools, not to mention regular internet access.

However, my dedication never wavered, and over the years, I've managed to hone my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and various frameworks and libraries such as Bootstrap, Tailwind, Sass, React, Node.js, Express.js, and Flask. Armed with this arsenal of skills, I've conjured up websites and web apps that not only work like a charm but also dazzle the digital senses.

But here's the thing: the learning never stops. Every day is a chance to level up, to sharpen my skills, to dive headfirst into the ever-shifting tide of web development. So, join me on this wild digital ride, where every click of the keyboard is a step toward crafting virtual experiences that'll leave you grinning from ear to ear.

Welcome to my world – let's make some web magic!
